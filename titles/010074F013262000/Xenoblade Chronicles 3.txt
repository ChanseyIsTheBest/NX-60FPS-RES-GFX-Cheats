Enable/Disable Checkerboard Rendering/Temporal Upsampling

This reverts the game to behave like 1DE and 2. You can choose TAA, FXAA, or no AA via lib_nx.bin, max res is 720p instead of upscaled 540p

By Hazerou, theboy181, MasaGratoR, OldManKain

60 FPS known Issues;

1.) 2D Elements and some Effects seem to run faster or not appear.

2.) You may need to use fpslocker and tweak the Window Sync Wait to fix some FPS Issues.

=====
 
Included in the text file;


[Handheld RRS 50% (360p)]
[Handheld RRS 66.66% (480p)]
[Handheld RRS 75% (544p)]
[Handheld RRS 85% (616p)]
[Default Handheld RRS 100% (720p)]
[Docked RRS 66.66% (712p)]
[Docked RRS 75% (816p)]
[Docked RRS 83.33% (896p)]
[Docked RRS 90% (976p)]
[Default Docked RRS 100% (1080p)]
[Disable RRS Lock (Re-Enable D.R.S.)]


Notes; 

1.) [RRS] is Render Resolution Scale and it disables Dynamic Resolution Function.
Please use;
Handheld RRS for Handheld Mode.
Docked RRS for Docked Mode.

2.) [RRS] may not work or may not be accurate if there's a Mod/Patch used such as Resolution/Graphics Mod etc.
